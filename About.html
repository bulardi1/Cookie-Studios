Cookie Studios

Cookie Studios is a small, but passionate team made up of three individuals: Uros Zbiljic, Lena Danijali, and Emilija Sladojevic. Although small in size, this team is big in talent and creativity. Uros Zbiljic is the programmer on the team, bringing his expertise in coding and development to the table. Lena Danijali and Emilija Sladojevic are both artists, lending their skills in design and illustration to the projects they work on. Together, they form a well-rounded team that is able to take on a variety of projects and deliver high-quality results. Despite their small size, Cookie Studios has a big vision. With Uroz, Lena, and Emilija at the helm, they aim to create innovative and engaging content that will captivate audiences and leave a lasting impression. Their passion for their work is evident in everything they do, and they are committed to delivering the best possible results for their clients. In a world where big teams and big budgets often dominate the creative landscape, Cookie Studios proves that small teams can make a big impact.
